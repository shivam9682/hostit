Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading healthcare institution in Northern India

30+ Years of Excellence in Healthcare

Established in 1992, Lucknow Hospital began as a 50-bed facility with a vision to provide accessible, quality healthcare to the people of Uttar Pradesh. What started as a small clinic has now transformed into a 500-bed multi-specialty hospital, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by some of the best medical professionals in the country.

Our journey has been guided by a simple yet powerful philosophy: every patient deserves compassionate care combined with medical excellence. Over the years, we have expanded our services, incorporated advanced medical technologies, and built a team of dedicated healthcare professionals who share our commitment to patient-centric care.

Today, we stand as a beacon of hope and healing, having treated over 2 million patients and performed more than 500,000 successful surgeries. Our commitment to innovation continues as we embrace new technologies and treatment methodologies to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our approach to healthcare

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, compassionate, and accessible healthcare services of the highest quality to all sections of society. We are committed to advancing medical knowledge through research, educating future healthcare professionals, and improving the health and well-being of our community through preventive care and health education.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare institution in Northern India, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and patient-centered care. We aspire to set new standards in medical education, research, and community health, creating a healthier society through our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Our Journey

Milestones in our three-decade journey of healthcare excellence

1992

Hospital Founded

Lucknow Hospital established as a 50-bed facility with basic medical services, focusing on providing affordable healthcare to the local community.

2001

Expansion to 200 Beds

Major expansion completed, increasing capacity to 200 beds and adding specialized departments including Cardiology and Orthopedics.

2010

Nursing Institute Established

Launched the Institute of Nursing & Paramedical Science to train future healthcare professionals, addressing the shortage of skilled medical staff.

2015

500-Bed Facility

Completed major renovation and expansion to become a 500-bed multi-specialty hospital with advanced diagnostic and treatment facilities.

2022

30th Anniversary

Celebrated 30 years of service, having treated over 2 million patients and performed more than 500,000 successful medical procedures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Lucknow Hospital

What specialties does Lucknow Hospital offer? +

We offer a comprehensive range of medical specialties including Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, Oncology, Gastroenterology, Nephrology, Urology, Gynecology, Endocrinology, Dermatology, Psychiatry, and General Medicine. Our multi-specialty approach ensures integrated care for all our patients.

Does Lucknow Hospital accept insurance? +

Yes, we have tie-ups with all major insurance providers in India including government schemes like Ayushman Bharat. Our insurance desk assists patients with documentation and claim processing. We also offer cashless treatment facilities for patients with valid insurance coverage.

How can I book an appointment? +

You can book appointments through multiple channels: 1) Online through our website/appointment portal 2) By calling our appointment helpline at +91 9682957154 3) Visiting our hospital reception in person 4) Through our mobile app (available on Play Store and App Store). We recommend booking in advance for specialist consultations.

Does the hospital provide emergency services? +

Yes, we have a 24/7 fully-equipped emergency department with dedicated trauma care units, ambulance services, and a team of emergency medicine specialists. Our emergency response team is trained to handle all types of medical emergencies including cardiac, neurological, trauma, and pediatric emergencies.

What are the visiting hours for patients? +

General visiting hours are from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM on weekends. However, we allow two attendants per patient who can stay 24/7. For ICU patients, we have specific visiting hours (usually 15 minutes, twice a day) to ensure patient safety and prevent infections.
