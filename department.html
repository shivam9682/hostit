Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our departments and services

How do I choose the right department for my condition? +

Our hospital reception and online consultation service can help guide you to the appropriate department. You can also use our online symptom checker or speak with our general physician who will refer you to the right specialist department based on your condition.

Do departments work 24/7? +

Emergency, Critical Care, and Trauma departments operate 24/7. Other departments have specific working hours but maintain emergency on-call services. Please check individual department pages for specific timings.

Can I get treatment from multiple departments? +

Yes, we offer integrated multi-disciplinary care. Our departments collaborate through the Hospital Information System to provide comprehensive treatment plans. Complex cases are managed through our Multi-Disciplinary Team (MDT) approach.

How do I book appointments for specialized departments? +

You can book appointments online through our website, mobile app, or by calling our centralized appointment helpline. For specialized departments requiring referrals, please consult with a general physician first.

Our expert doctors and state-of-the-art facilities are ready to provide you with the best medical care. Schedule your consultation today.
